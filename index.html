About Us

We are team 12717, AKA JagBotics, from Johnson High School in San Antonio, Texas! Our team meets 1-2 times per week throughout the school year to build and program a robot to participate in the First Tech Challenge, where we have been competing since 2019!

The First Tech Challenge

FTC is an international competition in which teams from across the globe design, build, and program robots to accomplish various goals. The robots will then compete against each other in a game that changes each season! Here, students don't just learn how to build robots. They also learn about teamwork, leadership, and gracious professionalism.
